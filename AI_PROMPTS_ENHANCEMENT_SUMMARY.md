# AI Research Prompts Enhancement Summary

## Overview
Enhanced AI research prompts for all 15 stages with significantly more contextual information to improve effectiveness and user outcomes.

## Enhancement Strategy

### What Was Added to Each Prompt:

#### 1. **Structured Context Section**
- Clear stage identification
- Purpose statement for the stage
- Connection to research journey

#### 2. **Detailed Profile Section**
- Role and experience level
- Available resources (time, budget, equipment)
- Technical skills and background
- Collaboration opportunities
- Career goals and constraints

#### 3. **Comprehensive "What I Need" Section**
- Numbered, specific requirements
- Sub-items with detailed expectations
- Clear deliverables for each item
- Quantifiable outputs where possible

#### 4. **Output Format Specifications**
- Specific formatting requirements
- Organization structure
- Visual elements needed (tables, charts, timelines)
- Level of detail expected

#### 5. **Quality Criteria**
- Success metrics
- Standards to meet
- Validation requirements
- Alignment with goals

## Stages Enhanced (So Far)

### ✅ Stage 1: Domain Discovery
**Enhancements:**
- Added profile fields: experience level, background, interests, constraints
- Specified output format: clear headings, examples, conferences, key researchers
- Added career prospects and time-to-productivity estimates
- Quality criteria: emerging domains, active community, feasibility

**Impact:** Users now provide complete context, get more targeted domain suggestions with career guidance

### ✅ Stage 2: Literature Review
**Enhancements:**
- Added research focus, target papers, timeline, database access
- Expanded requirements: 10-15 queries with Boolean operators, reading order strategy
- Added 4-week reading plan requirement
- Quality criteria: query coverage, author activity, paper citations

**Impact:** Users get comprehensive search strategy with organized reading plan

### ✅ Stage 3: Research Gap Identification
**Enhancements:**
- Added literature review summary section
- Detailed NIFI evaluation with 3-level scoring
- Added evidence of gap, impact assessment, resource requirements
- Risk level assessment and validation strategy
- Next steps for top 3 gaps

**Impact:** Users get validated, ranked gaps with clear action plans

### ✅ Stage 4: Research Implementation
**Enhancements:**
- Added research project details and expected contribution
- Comprehensive sprint planning with dependencies
- Risk management and contingency plans
- Reproducibility checklist
- Weekly progress tracking template

**Impact:** Users get realistic, detailed implementation roadmap with risk mitigation

### ✅ Stage 5: Research Paper Drafting
**Enhancements:**
- Added paper type, page limit, deadline, current status
- Detailed research summary with metrics
- Section-by-section writing strategy
- Figure/table design guidelines
- Venue-specific requirements
- Pre-submission checklist

**Impact:** Users get complete paper writing guide tailored to target venue

### ✅ Stage 6: Patent Drafting
**Enhancements:**
- Added innovation details, prior art knowledge
- Patentability assessment framework
- Geographic strategy and cost-benefit analysis
- Claims drafting guidance with structure
- Prior art search strategy
- Cost and timeline breakdown

**Impact:** Users get comprehensive patent filing strategy with cost analysis
