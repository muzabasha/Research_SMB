### Phase 2: Execution (Stages 4-6)
**Enhanced from placeholders to full content**

#### Stage 4: Research Implementation
- **Transformation**: Theory → Practice
- **Duration**: 6-12 months
- **Key Learning**: Break into 2-week sprints, document everything, test incrementally
- **Do's**: Weekly supervisor meetings, research journal, incremental testing
- **Don'ts**: Disappearing for months, skipping documentation, ignoring negative results
- **Success Story**: Amit Kumar - 24 sprints, finished in 10 months

#### Stage 5: Research Paper Drafting
- **Transformation**: Doer → Communicator
- **Duration**: 2-3 months
- **Key Learning**: IMRaD structure, 500 words daily, peer review before submission
- **Do's**: Daily writing, follow guidelines, use reference manager
- **Don'ts**: Waiting for perfect results, plagiarism, ignoring formatting
- **Success Story**: Divya Menon - 30-day writing challenge, paper accepted

#### Stage 6: Patent Drafting
- **Transformation**: Researcher → Innovator
- **Duration**: 1-2 months
- **Key Learning**: File BEFORE publishing, prior art search, patent attorney review
- **Do's**: Detailed documentation, provisional patent first, international filing
- **Don'ts**: Publishing before filing, copying claims, skipping attorney review
- **Success Story**: Dr. Rajesh Iyer - Lost IP first time, learned lesson

### Phase 3: Funding (Stages 7-9)
**Enhanced from placeholders to full content**

#### Stage 7: Seed Money Proposal
- **Transformation**: Dependent → Proposer
- **Duration**: 2-3 weeks
- **Key Learning**: SMART objectives, realistic budget, clear deliverables
- **Do's**: Read successful proposals, align with priorities, detailed budget
- **Don'ts**: Inflating budget, copy-pasting, ignoring guidelines
- **Success Story**: Dr. Kavita Sharma - ₹2L seed → ₹25L national grant

#### Stage 8: National Funding Proposal
- **Transformation**: Local → National
- **Duration**: 1-2 months
- **Key Learning**: Target SERB/DST/ICMR/DBT, highlight national relevance
- **Do's**: Study funded proposals, show publications, institutional endorsement
- **Don'ts**: Applying without publications, generic proposals, missing resubmission
- **Success Story**: Dr. Suresh Patel - 3 attempts, got ₹35L SERB grant

#### Stage 9: International Funding Proposal
- **Transformation**: National → International
- **Duration**: 2-3 months
- **Key Learning**: NSF/EU/NIH, international collaborations, global challenges
- **Do's**: Partner internationally, address SDGs, unique Indian context
- **Don'ts**: No international network, ignoring cultural differences, IP clauses
- **Success Story**: Dr. Meera Krishnan - MIT collaboration, €500K EU grant

### Phase 4: Lab Development (Stages 10-12)
**Enhanced from placeholders to full content**

#### Stage 10: Seed Lab Setup
- **Transformation**: Individual → Team Leader
- **Duration**: 3-6 months
- **Key Learning**: Lab layout, equipment procurement, safety protocols, team building
- **Do's**: Prioritize essentials, plan expansion, SOPs from day 1
- **Don'ts**: Buying without training, ignoring safety, micromanaging
- **Success Story**: Dr. Arun Desai - Learned to distribute budget wisely

#### Stage 11: Preliminary Research to TRL 1-3
- **Transformation**: Theory → Prototype
- **Duration**: 6-12 months
- **Key Learning**: TRL framework, proof-of-concept, prototype testing
- **Do's**: Start simple, test early, document experiments, measure metrics
- **Don'ts**: Aiming for perfection, skipping baselines, ignoring failures
- **Success Story**: Dr. Lakshmi Narayan - 7 prototypes, learned from failures

#### Stage 12: Extended Lab with Major Funding
- **Transformation**: Small → Scaled
- **Duration**: 12-24 months
- **Key Learning**: Manage large grants, scale team to 10+, advanced equipment
- **Do's**: Hire diverse team, training, clear roles, collaborative culture
- **Don'ts**: Expanding too fast, micromanaging, neglecting reporting
- **Success Story**: Dr. Venkat Raman - ₹2Cr grant, 12 people, 10 papers/year

### Phase 5: Excellence (Stages 13-15)
**Enhanced from placeholders to full content**

#### Stage 13: Tangible Outcomes & Industry Collaboration
- **Transformation**: Academic → Applied
- **Duration**: 12-18 months
- **Key Learning**: TRL 6-9, industry partnerships, technology transfer, revenue
- **Do's**: Identify pain points, clear IP agreements, win-win partnerships
- **Don'ts**: Giving away IP, ignoring market needs, working with one company
- **Success Story**: Dr. Anita Deshmukh - 5 companies, ₹50L revenue, 8 patents

#### Stage 14: Center of Excellence Establishment
- **Transformation**: Lab → Institution
- **Duration**: 24-36 months
- **Key Learning**: CoE vision, governance, sustainability, national reputation
- **Do's**: Clear focus, diverse board, multiple revenue streams, 10-year plan
- **Don'ts**: CoE just for name, single funding source, ignoring politics
- **Success Story**: Dr. Ramesh Kumar - 3 to 25 people, ₹5Cr budget, national CoE

#### Stage 15: Academic Program Launch
- **Transformation**: Researcher → Educator
- **Duration**: 12-24 months
- **Key Learning**: NEP 2020 curriculum, program governance, faculty recruitment
- **Do's**: Multidisciplinary, research integration, industry internships, 80% hands-on
- **Don'ts**: Copying programs, theory-heavy, neglecting soft skills
- **Success Story**: Dr. Priya Nair - M.Tech AI & Robotics, 100% placement
