#### Step 2: Problem Builder
- **Educational Section Added**: "What Makes a Good Research Problem?"
- **Content**:
  - Clear explanation of problem statement components
  - Formula for writing problem statements

#### Step 3: Literature Hunt
- **Educational Section Added**: "Why Literature Review is Critical"
- **Content**:
  - Importance of understanding existing research

  - Guidance on identifying research gaps and contradictions

- **Educational Section Added**: "Methodology: Your Research Blueprint"
- **Content**:

  - Clear definitions of each methodology element

#### Step 5: Outcome Path
- **Educational Section Added**: "Planning for Impact"
- **Content**:
  - Explanation of research impact beyond answering questions
  - Five types of research impact (Academic, Practical, Social, Economic, Environmental)
  - Encouragement to think about beneficiaries
  - Connection between research and positive change

## 🎨 Design Patterns Used

### Educational Content Boxes
All educational sections follow a consistent design pattern:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────┐
│ 💡 [Title]                          │
│ ─────────────────────────────────── │
│ [Main explanation paragraph]        │
│                                     │
│ ┌─────────────────────────────┐   │
│ │ [Subsection Title]          │   │
│ │ • Bullet point 1            │   │
│ │ • Bullet point 2            │   │
│ │ • Bullet point 3            │   │
│ └─────────────────────────────┘   │
└─────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Visual Features**:
- Colored left border (blue, purple, green, orange, indigo)
- Light background matching border color
- Icon/emoji in title
- White nested box for tips/examples
- Consistent spacing and typography

## 📚 Educational Content Strategy

### Principles Applied

1. **Progressive Disclosure**
   - Information revealed at the right time
   - Context-specific guidance
   - Not overwhelming beginners

2. **Practical Examples**
   - Real-world problem statement example
   - Concrete tips and formulas
   - Actionable advice

3. **Encouragement & Confidence Building**
   - Positive language throughout
   - "You can do this" messaging
   - Celebrating progress

4. **Clear Structure**
   - Consistent formatting
   - Visual hierarchy
   - Easy to scan

5. **Relevance**
   - Content tied to current step
   - Domain-specific when applicable
   - Addresses common concerns

- **Encouraging**: Build confidence
