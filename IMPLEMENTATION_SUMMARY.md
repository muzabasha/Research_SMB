**Step 2 - Problem Builder**

**Step 3 - Literature Hunt**

- What to look for in papers

**Step 4 - Experiment Builder**

- Key components breakdown
- Clear definitions of hypothesis, variables, procedure

**Step 5 - Outcome Path**
- "Planning for Impact" section
- Five types of research impact
- Connection to positive change

#### Added Welcome Banner
- Professional yet supportive tone
- Three value cards: Smart Matching, Success Insights, Build Legacy

#### Educational Content for Each Tab

**Profile Tab**

- Four-step beginner pathway
- Clear explanations for each step
- Motivational content
