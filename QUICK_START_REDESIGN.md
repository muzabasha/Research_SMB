## 📚 Sample Activities (Ready to Use!)

### 1. Domain Dating (Stage 1)
**Duration**: 90 minutes
**Format**: Speed dating with 5 research domains
**Outcome**: Choose your research domain with passion

**Activity Flow**:
- Pre-assessment (10 min)
- Orientation (30 min)
- Speed dating rounds (30 min) - 5 min per domain
- Deep dive (20 min) - explore top 2 domains
- Reflection & commitment (20 min)
- Post-assessment (10 min)

### 2. Paper Bingo (Stage 2)
**Duration**: 2 hours
**Format**: Game-based paper reading
**Outcome**: Learn to read papers critically

**Activity Flow**:
- Pre-assessment (10 min)
- Orientation (30 min)
- Paper Bingo game (20 min)
- Critical reading challenge (25 min)
- Gap matrix creation (15 min)
- Reflection (20 min)
- Post-assessment (10 min)

### 3. Gap Hunting (Stage 3)
**Duration**: 90 minutes
**Format**: Detective game + pitch competition
**Outcome**: Identify validated research gap

**Activity Flow**:
- Pre-assessment (10 min)
- Orientation (30 min)
- Gap detective game (20 min)
- Gap validation workshop (25 min)
- Gap pitch competition (15 min)
- Reflection (20 min)
- Post-assessment (10 min)
